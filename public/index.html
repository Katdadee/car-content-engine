📚 The Playbook — What's Working Right Now

Use these alongside your 40-Day Plan. The day-by-day calendar gives you the what; the playbook gives you the how — content formats that are converting in Clarksville right now, hooks that stop the scroll, and a fallback when you genuinely have nothing to post.

🎬 Best-Performing Content Types

  • Reels: home walkthroughs, POV videos, "Come tour this home," before/after transformations
  • Carousels: tips, buyer mistakes, neighborhood comparisons, "What X price gets you"
  • Stories: polls, Q&As, open house reminders, behind the scenes

🎣 High-Engagement Hooks

  • "You're probably overpaying in rent…"
  • "Nobody tells buyers this…"
  • "Would you live here for this price?"
  • "This home is ONLY 35 minutes to Nashville 👀"
  • "$10k incentives available right now 💰"
  • "Wait until you see the primary bathroom…"

💡 Stuck With Nothing to Post?

  • Talk about the market
  • Teach one thing
  • Show one home feature
  • Share one client story
  • Show your lifestyle / behind the scenes

✨ The North Star

  • Consistency beats perfection in real estate content.
  • Post even when it's not polished.
  • Your voice is your differentiator.
  • Build trust, not just impressions.
